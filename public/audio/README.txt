VOICE LINES — BRING YOUR OWN AUDIO
==================================

The app ships silent. Drop MP3 clips into this folder using the exact
filenames below and they will play automatically when the dragon reaches
the place (or the moment happens). Missing files are skipped gracefully —
add as few or as many as you like.

Use only audio you have the rights to (clips you own, licensed sound
packs, or recordings you made). Short clips work best: 3–20 seconds,
they duck the ambient soundscape and show a subtitle while playing.

LOCATION TRIGGERS
  mordor.mp3       Barad-dûr / the Black Land          (dark speech, rumbling)
  moria.mp3        the Gates of Moria                   (a wizard's defiance)
  shire.mp3        Hobbiton                             (warm, homely themes)
  rivendell.mp3    the Hidden Valley                    (elven song)
  lorien.mp3       Lothlórien                           (a whispered greeting)
  erebor.mp3       the Lonely Mountain                  (a dragon stirring)
  gondor.mp3       Minas Tirith                         (horns, trumpets)
  rohan.mp3        Edoras / the plains of Rohan         (riders, fiddle themes)
  weathertop.mp3   Amon Sûl                             (a cold shriek)
  isengard.mp3     Orthanc                              (a voice of iron)
  havens.mp3       the Grey Havens                      (gulls, farewells)

MOMENT TRIGGERS
  intro.mp3        plays once as the journey begins
  beacons.mp3      plays when all three beacons are lit
  complete.mp3     plays when all five lands are charted

Each location line replays only after you leave the area and return
(minimum 2 minutes between plays). Moment triggers play once per session.
